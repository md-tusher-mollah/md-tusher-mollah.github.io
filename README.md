# Dr. Md. Tusher Mollah

Academic portfolio website of **Dr. Md. Tusher Mollah**, Assistant Professor in Mathematics at the Department of Natural Sciences, Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Bangladesh.

Research areas include computational fluid dynamics (CFD), additive manufacturing, composite manufacturing, 3D concrete printing, rheology, porous-media flow, multiphysics simulation, and off-Earth construction.
